Air Quality

Making weather personal

Air quality in UTHM changes significantly in just a matter of hours and at a much higher resolution than the weather.
Realtime D-MON allows you to keep yourself notified during the day of varying air quality levels.

Health

Major health issue, economic burden

Air pollution has now become the biggest environmental concern raised by students in UTHM over the years.
Realtime D-MON recommends ways to minimize the exposure, guarantees a more personal experience and leads to healthier lifestyle.

Realtime D-MON Origins

What happens behind the scene.

Our mission is to improve the health and quality of life for UTHM students, by providing accurate air quality data in a format as simple, intuitive and actionable as weather data.

The project itself is developed as an Undergraduate Final Year Project and is still in beta mode. The source is hosted in Github and released under the
